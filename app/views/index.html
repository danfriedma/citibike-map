Compare the flow of citibike traffic in New York on an average weekday to an average weekend.

Inspired by the data visualization of Bradley Voytek at Uber and with the help of Mike Bostock's D3.

Each circle lies at the center of a neighborhood, outlined in black. A circle's radius is proportional to the number of rides that started in that neighborhood. Lines represent rides from one neighborhood to another. Line color indicates ride origin and line thickness indicates the relative number of riders who started in that neighborhood.
